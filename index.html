Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best app for 11+ exam preparation?

Cognithix is the only 11+ preparation app that covers all four tested areas: English, Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning, and the Language Aptitude Test (LAT). It features adaptive difficulty, unlimited on-device question generation, and a parent dashboard. Available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.

What is a good Bloomberg Terminal alternative for mobile?

Kanesh is a mobile financial data terminal where every number is source-linked to its SEC filing or data source. It features an interactive 3D globe covering 53 countries, on-device AI, natural language queries, and a Congress trading tracker. Available on the App Store.

What is the best maths app for primary school children in the UK?

Arithmetix is aligned to the UK National Curriculum from Reception to Year 6. It covers 30+ topics including times tables 2–12, uses adaptive difficulty that adjusts to your child's accuracy, and includes a virtual pet for motivation. No ads, no tracking.

What is a safe creative app for kids with no ads?

Sparks Studios is an AI-powered creative playground where children draw, write stories, and compose music. All AI runs on-device — nothing is uploaded to any cloud. No ads, no tracking, no account required. Designed for ages 4–12 with age-adapted content.

Are BFG apps safe for children's privacy?

Yes. All BFG apps process data on-device only. No data is uploaded to any server. No accounts are required. No analytics or telemetry is collected. The apps contain no ads and no third-party tracking.
