Frequently Asked Questions

How does cycle detection work?

When you log bleeding after 3+ days without, Cyla recognises a new cycle start and closes the previous one automatically.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored on your device only. No accounts, no cloud, no servers. Deleting the app permanently deletes all data.

How accurate are predictions?

Predictions improve with more data. Cyla starts with a baseline and refines after 3-6 cycles.

Can I add past data?

Yes. Use the date selector in the daily log.

Apple Watch?

Compatible watches send wrist temperature via Apple Health for ovulation confirmation.

What's free?

Period tracking, daily symptom logging, cycle forecasting, calendar, and irregularity notifications.

What's premium?

One-time purchase: contraception side effect analysis, fertility tracking, health pattern insights, migraine monitoring, and data export.

How do I delete my data?

Use "Delete All Data" in settings. This is permanent. No server deletion is needed because no data is stored on any server.
