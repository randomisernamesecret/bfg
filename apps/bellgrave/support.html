Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of game is Bellgrave?

Bellgrave is a horde survival game. You choose a hero, fight waves of enemies, collect upgrades, and defeat bosses across an escalating run. Think Vampire Survivors in a dark 3D cathedral setting.

How do I unlock new heroes?

Heroes unlock by completing achievements during runs. Each hero has unique weapons, stats, and a signature ability.

What are reliquaries?

Reliquaries are rare caches that appear during specific time windows in a run. Discovering one permanently unlocks a passive bonus for all future runs.

How does the evolution system work?

When your weapon and a specific passive upgrade reach the right level, your weapon evolves into a more powerful form. Each hero has a unique evolution path.

Where is my save data stored?

All game data is stored on your device only. No accounts, no cloud, no servers. Deleting the app permanently deletes all progress.

Does the game contain flashing lights?

Yes. Bellgrave contains flashing visual effects during combat. A photosensitivity warning is displayed on the title screen before gameplay begins.

Does the game work on iPad and Mac?

Yes. Bellgrave supports iPhone, iPad, and Mac (via Mac Catalyst). The interface adapts to each screen size.

Are there in-app purchases?

The game may include optional one-time purchases. There are no subscriptions, no ads, and no pay-to-win mechanics.
