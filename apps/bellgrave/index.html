What It Is

Horde Survival

Waves of enemies close in from every direction. Move, dodge, and let your auto-firing weapon clear the path. Each run escalates from a few stragglers to hundreds on screen.

3D Cathedral Setting

Fight through a dark cathedral rendered in full 3D with SceneKit. Altars, coffins, braziers, and ruins frame the battlefield with a Diablo IV atmosphere.

11 Playable Heroes

Each hero has a unique weapon behavior, base stats, and signature ability. Seraphine's wand, Lucien's rapid knives, Bram's sweeping chains, Nyx's homing orbs, and more.

Weapon Evolutions

Combine your weapon with the right passive upgrade to evolve it into a more powerful form. Each hero has a unique evolution path.

8 Boss Fights

From the Ash Bishop at 4 minutes to the Eclipse Warden at 13.5 minutes. Each boss has shields, special attacks, wind-up tells, and vulnerability windows.

Daily Challenges

A new seeded run every day with a rotating hero. Compare your performance across attempts and push for higher kill counts.

No Subscription. No Ads. No BS.

  • One-time unlock. Buy it once, play it forever.
  • No ads, no pop-ups, no video rewards to sit through.
  • No battle pass, no season pass, no FOMO timers.
  • No energy system. Run as many times as you want.
  • No pay-to-win. Every hero and upgrade is earned in-game.
  • Plays fully offline — no account, no login, no sign-up wall.

Progression

Signature Abilities

Every hero has a unique signature that activates automatically during combat. Seraphine's moonlight shot, Bram's gravity pull, Mordecai's blood orbs, and 8 more.

Passive Upgrades

17 passive upgrades across weapon stats, survivability, economy, and mobility. Level them up during runs to shape your build.

Achievements & Unlocks

Complete challenges to unlock new heroes, earn coins, and discover reliquaries that grant permanent bonuses.

Eclipse Phases

The eclipse cycle shifts between Calm, Surge, Ebb, and Eclipse phases, changing enemy intensity and run rhythm.
