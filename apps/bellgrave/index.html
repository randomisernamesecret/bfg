Survive, then keep going

Waves close in from every direction, your weapon auto-fires, and each run escalates from a handful of stragglers to hundreds on screen. Read the telegraphs, Phase Step through them, and push past the final boss into Overtime Crescendo until you're overwhelmed.

New in 2.0 — Seeded

A horde-survivor that finally rewards skill, not just movement — with builds that diverge, a difficulty ladder for mastery, and seeds you can hand to a friend.

Phase Step & Perfect Dodge

An active i-frame dodge with weight. Time it through a boss's telegraph and land a Perfect Dodge for a reward — the game now pays out skill, not just running in circles.

Builds that diverge

The upgrade pool was rebuilt from convergent to divergent. Synergy passives, plus reroll, banish and Lock on the level-up offer, mean two runs of the same hunter play nothing alike.

Curse Depths

A per-hunter stacking ascension ladder from Surface to Nightfall. Clear a depth, unlock the next, and chase mastery as the run gets meaner.

Mastery Keystones

Permanent per-hunter perks that keep growing even when you lose. A bad run still moves you forward.

Overtime Crescendo

Killing the final boss no longer ends the run. The horde keeps coming — push your build until you're finally overwhelmed.

Hunter's Chronicle

A completion grid where every run ticks a box, backed by a Nemesis grudge list that remembers what put you down.

Pay once. No ads. No timers.

Bellgrave is a premium game that respects your wallet. Buy it once and it's yours — nothing stands between you and the next run, and nothing nags you mid-fight.

  • One-time unlock — buy it once, play it forever.
  • No ads, no pop-ups, no video rewards to sit through.
  • No battle pass, no season pass, no FOMO timers.
  • No energy system — run as many times as you want.
  • No pay-to-win — every hunter and upgrade is earned in-game.
  • Everything on-device — no account, no login, no sign-up wall.

Bragging rights, built in

Every run feeds a longer game — and now you can hand the exact same run to a friend and dare them to do better.

Seed-share runs

Share a seed code or QR and a friend drops into the exact same setup — same dungeon, same horde, same loot. Outplay me.

End-of-run brag cards

Every run ends with a shareable card stamping your hunter, depth and kill count. Proof, not trash talk.

11 hunters

Eleven hunters, each with a unique weapon, signature and feel. One-time unlock — no ads, no subscription.

More dark biomes

The cathedral was only the beginning. 2.0 opens up new dark environments to fight your way through.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a subscription?

No. Bellgrave is a one-time unlock with no subscription, no ads, no battle pass and no energy timers.

What's new in 2.0?

The Seeded update adds the Phase Step dodge with Perfect-Dodge rewards, divergent builds, the Curse Depths ascension ladder, Mastery Keystones, Overtime Crescendo, the Hunter's Chronicle, new dark biomes, and seed-share runs.

How does seed-share work?

Share a seed code or QR and a friend gets the exact same run — same dungeon, same horde, same loot — then compare brag cards and outplay each other.
