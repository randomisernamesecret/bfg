Waves close in from every direction, your weapon auto-fires, and each run escalates from a handful of stragglers to hundreds on screen. Move, dodge, evolve.

What it is

Vampire-Survivors-style horde survival in a full 3D cathedral, with the build depth of a roguelike and the presentation of an ARPG.

Horde survival

Enemies close in from every direction. Your weapon auto-fires — your job is to move, dodge, and clear the path as each run escalates to hundreds on screen.

Dark 3D cathedral

Fight through a fully 3D cathedral of altars, coffins, braziers and ruins, framed in a brooding Diablo-style atmosphere.

11 playable heroes

Each hero has a unique weapon, base stats and signature ability — Seraphine's wand, Lucien's rapid knives, Bram's sweeping chains, Nyx's homing orbs, and more.

Weapon evolutions

Pair your weapon with the right passive upgrade to evolve it into a stronger form. Every hero has its own evolution path.

8 boss fights

From the Ash Bishop early on to the Eclipse Warden near the end — each boss has shields, special attacks, wind-up tells, and vulnerability windows.

Daily challenges

A fresh seeded run every day with a rotating hero. Compare attempts and push for higher kill counts.

Pay once. No subscription. No ads.

Bellgrave is a premium game. Buy it once and it's yours — no meters between you and the next run, no store nagging you mid-fight.

  • One-time unlock — buy it once, play it forever.
  • No ads, no pop-ups, no video rewards to sit through.
  • No battle pass, no season pass, no FOMO timers.
  • No energy system — run as many times as you want.
  • No pay-to-win — every hero and upgrade is earned in-game.
  • Plays fully offline — no account, no login, no sign-up wall.

Progression

Every run feeds a longer game — shape a build in the moment, then unlock heroes and permanent bonuses across attempts.

Signature abilities

Every hero has a signature that fires automatically in combat — Seraphine's moonlight shot, Bram's gravity pull, Mordecai's blood orbs, and 8 more.

Passive upgrades

17 passive upgrades across weapon stats, survivability, economy and mobility. Level them up mid-run to shape your build.

Achievements & unlocks

Complete challenges to unlock new heroes, earn coins, and discover reliquaries that grant permanent bonuses.

Eclipse phases

The eclipse cycle shifts between Calm, Surge, Ebb and Eclipse phases, changing enemy intensity and the rhythm of each run.
