Cloudmesh Weather

Privacy Policy Support Terms of Use

Cloudmesh is published by Blue Flame Games ("BFG", "we", "us"). We collect as little as possible and we cannot identify you.

Data Collected

  • Barometric readings — pressure, coordinates, altitude. Retained for 6 hours for the mesh network.
  • Weather reports — conditions and location. Retained for 24 hours.
  • Seismic events — acceleration and confidence. Retained for 1 hour. Beta feature.
  • Push tokens — device token and approximate location. Stored until expiration.

Location used for weather display is not transmitted except within the above readings.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Device IDs, user IDs, or accounts
  • IDFA
  • Contact details
  • Usage analytics
  • IP logs
  • Cookies or fingerprinting
  • No third-party analytics or ad SDKs

Anonymity

No login is required. Non-persistent random IDs are used. Location is coarsened. Data types are isolated. Data is auto-purged within hours — only aggregates persist.

Push Notifications

Your push token and approximate location are stored on our server. No identity is linked. Disable push notifications via iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Weather data providers receive coordinates only.
  • Earthquake and environmental data comes from public feeds.
  • Apple processes in-app purchases.

In-App Purchases

One-time purchases via StoreKit 2. We do not receive any payment information.

Children

No personal information is collected from any user.

Data Storage

Data is stored in Europe, protected by TLS, private networks, and rate limiting.

User Rights

No personal data or accounts exist, so access, correction, and deletion requests are not applicable. Readings are auto-deleted.
