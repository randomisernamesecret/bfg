Cloudmesh Weather

29 widgets. Community mesh. One purchase.

Privacy Policy Support Terms of Use

Support

Need help with Cloudmesh? Email us at blueflamegames@proton.me and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Free Features

  • Current conditions
  • Hourly and daily forecasts
  • Umbrella widget
  • AI weather summary
  • Rain notifications (2 cities)
  • Mesh contribution
  • Home and lock screen widgets

Premium (One-Time Purchase)

  • All 29 widgets
  • Unlimited cities
  • All app icons
  • Minute-by-minute precipitation
  • Commute weather
  • Air quality
  • Earthquake alerts
  • Outfit and activity suggestions
  • Custom widget profiles per city

Good to Know

  • No account needed.
  • Mesh = anonymous sensor data from the community.
  • Earthquake detection is a beta feature.
  • Restore purchases via your Apple ID.
  • Mesh contribution can be disabled in settings.

Requirements

iOS 18+. Supported sensors. Location Services and internet connection required.

Limitations

  • Minute-by-minute precipitation is geographically restricted.
  • Earthquake detection is experimental.
  • Air quality data availability varies by region.
