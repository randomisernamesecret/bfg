Metabase, but in your pocket

Dashpocket is a native iOS companion for Metabase, the open-source BI tool. Point it at your own Metabase instance and your dashboards and questions become fast, glanceable, native charts — the kind you can read in a couple of seconds between meetings. Nothing about your numbers passes through a Blue Flame Games server, because there isn't one.

Native charts

Your Metabase questions rendered as fast Swift charts. Tap and scrub for exact values, or swap any chart for the underlying table.

On-device AI briefing

A plain-language read on what your numbers are doing, written on the device in your language. Nothing leaves the phone.

Smart alerts

Seasonality-aware anomaly detection that flags the genuinely unusual — delivered as local notifications, not a flood of noise.

Goals & forecasting

Set targets on the metrics that matter and see where the trend is heading, on device.

Read-only, by design

Dashpocket never edits your Metabase content. It connects, reads the dashboards and questions you already have, and presents them beautifully — so a phone glance answers the question instead of opening a laptop.

Tap & scrub

Touch any chart for the exact value at any point, then swap it for the underlying table when you want the raw rows.

Widgets & glances

Home-screen widgets and lock-screen glances keep your headline KPIs one look away.

Offline & locked

Offline caching keeps your last numbers available with no signal, and Face ID keeps the app sealed until it's you.

The news module

The numbers tell you what changed. The News module helps you understand why. Follow the subjects that matter to your business — your industry, your competitors, the topics you watch — and read the relevant public headlines in a clean, distraction-free reader, right next to the metrics they affect.

Follow subjects

Your subjects stay yours

The subjects you follow are stored on your device and only ever sent as a public news search query. Blue Flame Games never sees them.

Where your data lives

This is the part that matters most. Dashpocket is built so your dashboards, credentials, and the numbers behind them stay yours.

No Blue Flame Games server

The app talks directly to your own Metabase over HTTPS. We never sit in the middle. There is no backend to breach because there is no backend.

Credentials in the Keychain

Your Metabase URL and credentials are stored only in the device Keychain — encrypted by iOS, never transmitted to us.

AI runs on the device

The briefings and explanations are generated locally. Your numbers are never sent off the phone to be summarised.

No accounts, no tracking

No sign-up, no analytics, no telemetry. Open the app, connect Metabase, and you're in.

Frequently asked questions

Is this an official Metabase app?

No. Dashpocket is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Metabase. It connects to your own instance.

Does my data reach your servers?

No. There is no Blue Flame Games server. The app talks directly to your Metabase, and the AI runs on the device.

Can it change my dashboards?

No. Dashpocket is read-only. It views your existing dashboards and questions — it never edits Metabase content.
