Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Lifecart is built to be private by design. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers.

Data we collect

None. Lifecart does not collect any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.

On-device content

Player names, game settings, your sound and language preferences, your Lifecart Plus unlock status, and any custom card packs you create are stored only on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

On-device AI

On supported devices, Lifecart uses Apple's on-device intelligence to generate game content. This processing happens entirely on your device. No prompts, game content, or results are sent to us or to any third party.

Custom packs

If you create a custom card pack and choose to export it, a file is created that you can share however you like (for example, via AirDrop or Messages). Sharing is entirely under your control; the file is not uploaded to us.

Purchases

Lifecart offers an optional one-time in-app purchase, "Lifecart Plus." Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment information. Apple's handling of purchases is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Children's privacy

Lifecart is rated 17+ for mature humor and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
