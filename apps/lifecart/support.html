Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lifecart?

Lifecart is a pass-and-play party game for 1–6 players. Everyone "shops" for an ideal life on a budget, each life prints as a receipt, and an on-device AI judge roasts every choice and crowns a winner.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Lifecart is fully offline. There are no accounts and no servers — everything runs on your device.

What does Apple Intelligence add?

On supported devices, Apple's on-device AI generates a fresh, never-repeating shop and the judge's roasts. On other devices, Lifecart uses a built-in deck of hand-written cards, so the full game is always playable.

What's the difference between Tame and Spicy?

Tame is family-friendly (PG-13). Spicy is adult, no-holds-barred humor for grown-up parties (17+). You choose the mode before each game.

What is Lifecart Plus?

Lifecart is free to play. Lifecart Plus is a one-time purchase that unlocks Spicy mode, all and custom themes, custom card-pack creation, and more players. No subscription, no ads.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings inside Lifecart and tap Restore Purchase. Your Lifecart Plus unlock is tied to your Apple Account.

How do custom packs work?

With Lifecart Plus you can create your own card packs and export them as a file. Anyone can import a shared pack file and play with it — importing is free.

How do I report a bug?

Email blueflamegames@proton.me with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.
