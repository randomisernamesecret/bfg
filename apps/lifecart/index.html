Lifecart

The pass-and-play party game where everyone shops for their ideal life on a budget — then an on-device AI judge reads each life aloud and roasts every questionable choice. Every shop is freshly generated, so no two games are the same.

One budget. Seven choices. Zero dignity.

Pick a Partner, a Career, a Friend Group, a Place to Live, a Companion, a Ride, and a Weekend Vibe — but you can't afford all the good stuff. Splurge on the dream house and you're dating a houseplant. Every life prints as a receipt, then the AI judge does the rest.

The loop is simple

Easy to learn at a glance, impossible to play with a straight face.

Stock the shop

Pick a budget and a theme. On capable devices, the on-device AI stocks a fresh, never-repeating shop every round.

Shop a life

Pass the phone around. Each player privately buys one option per aisle without blowing the budget.

Print the receipt

Every life prints as a torn-edge receipt with line items, stat bars, and a barcode — ready to share.

Get roasted

The AI judge crowns a winner, hands out superlatives, and roasts every life out loud.

Two modes for any room

Switch the humor to fit the table — and keep the laughs landing in any language.

  • Tame — family-friendly, great for game night with the kids.
  • Spicy — adult, no-holds-barred humor for grown-up parties (17+).
  • Make and share your own card packs — export a file, friends import it.
  • Fully localized in English, French, German, Spanish, Japanese, Chinese and Hindi.

Private by design

No accounts, no serversPlay on the couch, in the car, anywhere — it's fully offline.
