Every run, in your pocket

Start a session, watch your pace and route update in real time, then review the full history once you're done. Everything stays on your device.

What it does

A complete tracker for runs, rides, walks and hikes — with smart training-load monitoring and standard exports, built so your workout history stays yours.

GPS run, ride, walk & hike

Track any outdoor activity with real-time pace, distance and heart rate from your Apple Watch or iPhone.

Apple Watch independent GPS

Leave your phone at home. PaceGrid records directly from your wrist with full GPS tracking.

Injury Guard

A training-load monitor that watches your acute-to-chronic workload ratio and flags when you're ramping up too fast.

Strava sync

Share individual workouts to Strava when you choose. Nothing uploads automatically.

GPX & FIT export

Export any workout in standard formats compatible with virtually any fitness platform.

Works offline

No internet connection needed. Your watch and GPS are all it takes to log a session.

Train smarter, stay healthy

PaceGrid doesn't just count your miles — it watches how quickly you're loading up, so you can push hard without running yourself into an injury.

  • Injury Guard tracks acute-to-chronic workload ratio.
  • Real-time pace, distance and heart rate while you move.
  • Full workout history with splits and trends.
  • Export to GPX or FIT — your data goes wherever you do.

Who it's for

PaceGrid is for runners, cyclists and hikers who want a GPS fitness tracker with no subscription — a Strava-friendly running app that records on your wrist and keeps your training history on your own device instead of a vendor cloud.
