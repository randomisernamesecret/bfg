Privacy-first fitness tracking. No account required. All data stays on your device.

Key Information

Data Storage

All data is stored on your iPhone and Apple Watch. Nothing goes to the cloud unless you connect Strava or enable iCloud sync.

Export

GPX and FIT formats, including bulk export.

Strava

Optional auto-upload of completed workouts. Analytics stay on your device.

Injury Guard

On-device training load and biometrics monitoring. Pre-workout risk scores, live alerts, and post-workout recovery guidance.

Offline

PaceGrid works completely offline, including GPS, analytics, and training plans. Map tiles can be downloaded for offline use.

Pricing

One-time purchase. All features included. No subscriptions.

Requirements

iOS 17.0+. Apple Watch with watchOS 10.0+ (optional). GPS and HealthKit access.
