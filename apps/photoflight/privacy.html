PhotoFlight

Your travel map from your photos

Privacy Policy Support Terms of Use

Overview

PhotoFlight is designed with privacy at its core. All photo processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data, photos, or location information.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your photos or their contents
  • We do not collect GPS or location data from your photos
  • We do not collect your travel history or trip information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties

On-Device Processing

PhotoFlight reads GPS metadata from your photo library to build your travel map. This processing happens entirely on your iPhone. No photo data, location data, or personal information ever leaves your device.

Photo Library Access

PhotoFlight requests access to your photo library to read GPS location metadata and creation dates from your photos. This data is used solely to detect your travel locations and is processed and stored locally on your device.

Location Services

PhotoFlight may request location access to help you set your home address during setup. This is optional and the data is stored only on your device.

In-App Purchases

PhotoFlight offers a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple's App Store.

Data Storage

All app data (travel history, location clusters, trip information) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not backed up to any external server.

Children's Privacy

PhotoFlight is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at blueflamegames@proton.me.
