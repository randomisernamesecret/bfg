Frequently Asked Questions

How does PhotoFlight work?

PhotoFlight reads the GPS metadata embedded in your photos to automatically detect where you've traveled. All processing happens on your device.

Why are some countries missing?

PhotoFlight can only detect locations from photos that have GPS data. Screenshots, photos received via messaging apps, and some camera photos may not have location information.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open any Pro feature and tap "Restore Purchases" on the upgrade screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I add flights manually?

Yes. PhotoFlight Pro lets you add flights manually with origin, destination, and dates.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
