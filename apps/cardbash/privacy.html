Card Bash

Creature card battles and pack collecting

Privacy PolicySupportTerms of Use

Card Bash is designed to keep gameplay data on your device. We do not run accounts, social profiles, advertising SDKs, or third-party analytics inside the app.

Data Stored Locally

  • Card collection and deck choices
  • Coins, gems, shards, quests, and battle progress
  • Settings such as music and sound effects
  • Local purchase grant state for one-time rewards

Purchases

Optional in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details.

What We Do Not Do

  • No third-party advertising
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No behavioural tracking
  • No account requirement
  • No sale of personal data

Deletion

Deleting the app removes local gameplay data from the device, subject to normal iOS backup behaviour.
