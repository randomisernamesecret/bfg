Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of game is Card Bash?

Card Bash is a creature card battler. You open packs, collect cards, build a battle deck, and play short arena route battles against AI opponents.

Does Card Bash support iPad?

Yes. Card Bash is designed for iPhone first and adapts to iPad layouts.

Where is my collection stored?

Your card collection, wallet, quests, settings, and battle progress are stored on your device.

Are there in-app purchases?

Yes. Optional consumable purchases are available in the Store tab. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the Store tab inside Card Bash and use Restore Purchases. Consumable purchases cannot always be restored after they are spent because Apple treats them as consumables.

How do I report a bug?

Email blueflamegames@proton.me with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.
