What players see first

Card Bash is built around moments that are easy to understand at a glance: a pack is ready, a new creature joins the collection, a deck gets stronger, and the next arena route asks for one more fight.

The loop is simple

Every system is there to support the same player journey: collect something exciting, make a decision, see your deck improve, and come back when the next pack or run is ready.

Open packs

Start from an empty collection, claim daily packs, and reveal original Bash Beasts across different rarity tiers.

Build a squad

Pick cards from your own collection, inspect stats and backstories, then choose who earns a place in battle.

Battle it out

Take your squad into quick, tactical fights where the right call beats brute force, and climb toward tougher rivals.

Grow favorites

Cards earn XP, level up, and can be improved over time so a favorite pull can stay relevant.

Fast battles, smart choices

Battles are quick but tactical — the right creature and a clever play beat raw power. Easy to pick up, with enough depth to keep every run interesting.

  • Quick fights you can finish in one sitting.
  • Smart matchups reward thinking over button-mashing.
  • Climb toward tougher rivals as you go.
  • Tap any card for its art, stats, and lore.

Collectible first, store second

The Store exists for optional boosts, but the core promise is the collection: art worth looking at, cards worth upgrading, and a clear reason to open the next pack. Duplicate pulls turn into useful currency so pack openings still move the account forward.

Progress is stored locally on your device. Optional purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.
