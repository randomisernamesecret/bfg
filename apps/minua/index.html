What It Does

Earn Screen Time

Children earn fun app access by spending time in learning apps chosen by their parent. Configurable ratios from 1:1 to 12:1.

Apple Screen Time API

Uses Apple's built-in FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity frameworks for reliable, OS-level app restrictions.

Allowed Hours

Parents set a daily time window (e.g. 3pm-8pm) when fun apps can be unlocked. Outside these hours, fun apps stay locked.

Daily Reset

The time bank resets at midnight each day. Children start fresh every morning with a clean balance.

PIN-Protected Settings

All parent settings are locked behind a 4-digit PIN with lockout after failed attempts. Children cannot change the rules.

No Account Required

No email, no sign-up, no cloud sync. Set up the app and hand the device to your child. Everything stays on-device.

Privacy & Data

  • All usage data stored locally with iOS encryption.
  • No accounts, no cloud, no servers.
  • Screen Time API runs entirely on-device via Apple's frameworks.
  • Nothing to subpoena, nothing to breach, nothing to sell.

Who It Is For

Minua is for parents searching for a screen time app for kids, a parental control app without tracking, or a better way to connect learning time with recreational screen time. It is especially relevant for families who dislike the feel of surveillance-heavy parental control software.

Why Choose Minua?

Earned screen time model

The value proposition is more specific than generic parental control: Minua reframes screen time as something children earn through useful activity.

Apple-native controls

Using Apple's own Screen Time frameworks gives the product a cleaner, more trustworthy story than many cross-platform monitoring apps.

Private by default

No account and no cloud means the app can appeal to privacy-conscious parents who do not want family routines or child data living on a vendor server.

Better framing for family use

Rewarding learning is easier to explain and live with than endless punishment loops around screen limits alone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Minua spy on children?

No. The page is intentionally positioned around on-device controls rather than tracking-heavy surveillance software.

Do children earn time by using learning apps?

Yes. That earned-time mechanic is the core product concept.

Will it need another family account?

No. Minua is designed around local setup and Apple system frameworks, not another cloud account to manage.
