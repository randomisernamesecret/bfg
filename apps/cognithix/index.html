What It Does

All 4 tested areas

English comprehension, Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning, and the Language Aptitude Test in one app.

Adaptive difficulty

Adjusts to each child's accuracy in real time. Raises the challenge where they excel, adds support where they struggle.

Unlimited on-device questions

Questions are generated on-device so the supply is effectively unlimited. No repeating the same problem set.

Parent dashboard

See mastery across all four subjects at a glance. Track accuracy over time and know exactly where to focus.

Virtual pet motivation

A virtual pet grows based on consistent effort, not just correct answers. Encourages a growth mindset during stressful preparation.

Progress tracking

Detailed per-subject breakdown shows which areas are strong and which need more practice before exam day.

Privacy & Data

  • Parent account via Firebase for cross-device sync
  • Child profiles are parent-controlled
  • Only aggregated session data uploaded — no per-question answers

Who It Is For

Cognithix is for families preparing for grammar school entry who want a clear 11+ preparation app instead of stitching together separate resources for English, verbal reasoning, non-verbal reasoning, and the LAT. It is particularly relevant if you are searching for an Atom Learning alternative or an 11+ app for iPhone.

Unlimited practice story
