Sparks Studios

AI-powered creative playground for kids

Privacy Policy Support Terms of Use

Sparks Studios is a creative app for children aged 4-12, published by Blue Flame Games ("BFG", "we", "us"). Your family's privacy is fundamental to how we build this app.

Core Commitment

Sparks Studios does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All creations and profile information stay on your device. There are no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Photos, drawings, stories, or music
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers

Data Stored on Device Only

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted:

  • Child profiles (display names, avatars, age brackets — no real names required)
  • User creations
  • App preferences
  • Subscription status flags

AI Features

All AI features run entirely on-device using local ML models. No prompts, text, or content ever reaches external servers.

iCloud Sync

iCloud sync is optional, off by default, and requires explicit opt-in. When enabled, syncing uses Apple CloudKit and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Sparks Studios includes no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, ad networks, or crash reporting services. The only third-party integration is Apple StoreKit for processing subscriptions.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

No child information is collected. No child accounts are required. There are no ads, no behavioural tracking, content safety filtering is built in, and parental controls are available.

Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data. Delete individual items or profiles within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data.
