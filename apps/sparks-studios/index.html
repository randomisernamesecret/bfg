What It Does

Drawing canvas

Freeform sketching with AI-powered prompts and suggestions. The AI assists without taking over.

Story builder

Write stories with AI assistance for structure and vocabulary. Offers ideas when a child gets stuck, rather than writing for them.

Music composer

Create melodies by placing notes and hearing them played back. AI suggests harmonies and rhythmic patterns to accept or ignore.

On-device AI

All AI processing happens locally. Nothing is uploaded to any server. No account creation required.

Age-adapted content

Three tiers (4-6, 7-9, 10-12) adjust the interface, prompts, vocabulary, and complexity. One app from Reception through primary school.

Parental controls

Math gate entry, session time limits, and a creation gallery. No social sharing, no public gallery, no stranger interaction.

Privacy & Data

  • Zero data collection — no accounts, no cloud, no analytics
  • All AI runs entirely on-device
  • COPPA compliant
  • No ads or tracking of any kind
