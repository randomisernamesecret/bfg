Frequently Asked Questions

What devices does Sparks Studios support?

iOS 17+. AI features require iPhone 15 Pro or an M1 iPad.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Is my child's data safe?

All data stays on your device. There is no cloud storage, no accounts, and no tracking.

How do I delete my child's data?

Delete profiles and creations within the app, or uninstall the app to remove everything.

How do I contact support?

Email blueflamegames@proton.me.
