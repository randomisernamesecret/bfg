Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kanesh?

Kanesh is a financial data terminal for iPhone and iPad. It brings together company fundamentals, filings, market data, and macro series, with every figure traceable back to its original source.

Where does the data come from?

Figures are sourced from public providers such as company filings and official macroeconomic releases. Each data point links back to the source it came from, so you can verify it yourself.

Does the AI assistant send my questions to a server?

No. The assistant runs entirely on-device. Your questions and watchlists are not sent to Blue Flame Games or any third party. Kanesh connects to the internet only to fetch public market data — it collects nothing about you.

Is there a free version?

Yes. Core market data, search, and watchlists are free. A one-time purchase unlocks the full terminal — no subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings inside Kanesh and tap "Restore Purchases". Your unlock is tied to your Apple Account and restores on any device signed in with it.

Which markets are covered?

Kanesh covers equities and ETFs across major global markets. If a ticker or series you rely on is missing, email us and we'll look into adding it.

The price or a figure looks wrong — what do I do?

Market data can lag or be revised at the source. Tap a figure to see where it came from, and if something still looks off, email us with the ticker and a screenshot.

Is Kanesh investment advice?

No. Kanesh is a data and research tool. It does not provide investment, financial, or tax advice, and nothing in the app is a recommendation to buy or sell any security.
