What It Does

Source-tagged financials

Every number links back to its original SEC filing, FRED series, or data provider. Tap any figure to see exactly where it came from.

Natural language queries

Ask questions in plain English like "What was Apple's gross margin last quarter?" On-device AI processes your query entirely on your phone.

3D Globe — 53 countries

Browse global equities through an interactive 3D globe. Coverage spans 53 countries, not just US markets.

Congress trading tracker

See which stocks members of the US Congress are buying and selling, sourced directly from mandatory disclosure filings.

On-device AI

All queries are processed locally. Your questions never leave the device and no account is required.

SEC EDGAR direct access

Pull 10-K, 10-Q, and 8-K filings straight from EDGAR. Primary-source data without the desktop-only frustration.

Privacy & Data

  • All queries processed on-device — nothing leaves your phone
  • API requests send only ticker symbols, no personal data
  • No account required
  • No analytics or tracking
