Kanesh

Source-linked financial data terminal

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

Privacy by design. Your queries are processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your search queries to any server.

What Stays on Your Device

  • Search queries (processed by on-device AI)
  • Watchlist tickers (stored in SwiftData)
  • Cached financial data
  • API keys (stored in UserDefaults)
  • App preferences

What Is Transmitted

Kanesh makes API requests to the following financial data providers to fetch market and economic data:

  • SEC EDGAR
  • FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data)
  • Alpha Vantage
  • Financial Modeling Prep
  • Financial Datasets
  • Yahoo Finance

These requests contain only the data parameters necessary for the query (ticker symbols, date ranges, series IDs). No personal information is transmitted — no name, email, device identifier, or location data.

Kanesh contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

On-Device AI

Kanesh uses a large language model that operates entirely on your device. Your questions never leave your phone.

Third-Party Data Providers

Financial data is sourced from third-party providers, each with their own privacy policies:

  • SEC EDGAR Privacy Policy
  • FRED Privacy Policy
  • Alpha Vantage Terms of Service
  • Financial Modeling Prep Privacy Policy
  • Financial Datasets Privacy Policy
  • Yahoo Finance Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Kanesh is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
