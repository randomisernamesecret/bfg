Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a child profile?

Open the app, sign in to your parent account, and tap the Add Child button on the home screen.

How do I change my child's year group?

Go to your child's dashboard (tap their card on the home screen), then tap the info icon in the top right to update their age.

How do I restore my purchase?

Arithmetix is a one-time purchase. Restore via the welcome screen or sign in with the same Apple ID you used when you first bought it.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account in the app, or email us at blueflamegames@proton.me. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

My child's progress isn't saving?

Ensure you have an internet connection when finishing a session. If the issue continues, please contact support at blueflamegames@proton.me.
