What it does

Scan, set a date, get on with your day. Custoda does the remembering — and the ingredient-checking — so you don't have to.

Expiry tracking

Scan a barcode or set a date in a single tap. Custoda reminds you before things go off, so less food and medicine goes to waste.

Allergen warnings at scan

Tell Custoda what you avoid. Scan a product and it flags your allergens, additives or ingredients instantly — before it goes in the basket.

Honest about uncertainty

When ingredient data is missing or incomplete, Custoda says 'unverified'. It never tells you a product is safe when it can't be sure.

Fast, even in bad light

Can't read the tiny printed date? Set it with one tap from quick-pick chips — today, a week, a month. No typing.

Rapid multi-scan

Scan a whole shop one item after another, then set all the expiry dates together in one place.

Smart storage

Custoda guesses the right spot — fridge, freezer, pantry or medicine cabinet — from what you scanned.

Private, not just promised

Most tracker apps want an account before you can log a thing, then sync it to someone's cloud. Custoda doesn't.

  • Everything stored locally on your device.
  • No account, no cloud, no servers.
  • The network is used only for anonymous barcode lookups.
  • Nothing to breach, nothing to sell.

Frequently asked questions

Does Custoda keep my data private?

Yes. Your shelf, your avoid list and your reminders never leave your device. Barcode lookups send only the barcode number — no identity, no account.

Can Custoda tell me a product is safe?

No. Custoda flags what's on your avoid list and warns when ingredient data is incomplete. Always check the packaging — Custoda is not medical advice.

What does it cost?

Custoda is a one-time purchase — no subscription. Allergen warnings and expiry reminders are always free; premium simply adds capacity.
