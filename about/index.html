Blue Flame Games

BFG builds privacy-first iPhone apps designed to be useful on day one, understandable on the web, and respectful of the person using them.

What We Build

BFG apps span weather, education, finance, fitness, health, travel, and kids categories. Some are tools for daily life. Others are deep specialist apps. The common thread is that they are designed to be easy to trust: clear pricing, minimal data collection, and product pages that explain what the app actually does.

How We Think About Privacy

Privacy at BFG is architectural, not just marketing language. Many apps process data on-device, avoid accounts where possible, and do not depend on third-party tracking SDKs or advertising networks.

  • We prefer one-time purchases or simple pricing over attention-hacking monetisation.
  • We explain where app data comes from and how it is handled.
  • We publish support, privacy, and terms pages for every shipped app.

Who Makes It

BFG content is published under the byline Daniel Hart. Articles on this site are written to help people compare apps, understand privacy tradeoffs, and decide which product is actually right for them.
